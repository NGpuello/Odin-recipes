Alfredo Pasta

Description

Alfredo pasta is a sumptuous Italian dish known for its indulgent and creamy sauce. It typically features fettuccine noodles bathed in a rich and velvety Alfredo sauce. This sauce is made from a luxurious blend of butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, resulting in a smooth and decadent flavor. The dish is often garnished with freshly ground black pepper and a sprinkle of fresh parsley, adding a touch of elegance. Alfredo pasta is a comfort food favorite that offers a delightful combination of buttery, creamy, and savory flavors, making it a timeless classic that's cherished around the world.

Ingredients

For the pasta

  • 2 ounces (340 grams) fettuccine pasta (or pasta of your choice)

For the Alfredo sauce

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • A pinch of freshly grated nutmeg (optional)
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, melt butter over low heat.
  3. Stir in heavy cream and simmer until it thickens slightly.
  4. Remove from heat and whisk in grated Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  5. Season with salt, black pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg (optional).
  6. Toss the cooked pasta in the creamy Alfredo sauce.
  7. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley (optional) and serve hot.
